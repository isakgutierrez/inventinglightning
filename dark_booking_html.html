Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

For weddings, I recommend booking 6-12 months in advance to secure your date. Portrait sessions can typically be scheduled 2-4 weeks out, depending on the season and availability.

Do you travel for shoots?

Yes! I'm available for travel throughout California and beyond. Travel fees apply for locations outside the LA area. International projects are also welcome with advance planning.

What's included in the session fee?

All session fees include the photography time, professional editing and retouching, and an online gallery for viewing and downloading your images. Print releases are included for personal use.

How long until I receive my photos?

Portrait sessions: 1-2 weeks. Weddings and events: 4-6 weeks. Commercial projects vary based on scope. Rush delivery is available for an additional fee when needed.

Can I get the raw files?

I provide professionally edited images as the final product, as these represent my artistic vision. Raw files are available for an additional fee and only after the edited gallery is delivered.

What's your cancellation policy?

Cancellations more than 7 days before the session are fully refundable. Within 7 days, 50% of the session fee is retained. Weather-related cancellations can be rescheduled without penalty.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, payment plans are available for bookings over $1,000. Typically a 50% deposit secures your date, with the remainder due on the day of the shoot.

What should I wear for a portrait session?

I provide a detailed style guide upon booking with recommendations based on your session type and location. Generally, solid colors and timeless styles work best for lasting appeal.
