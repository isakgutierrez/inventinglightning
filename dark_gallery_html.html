Urban Solitude

An intimate exploration of isolation and connection within metropolitan spaces. This ongoing series captures quiet moments of human experience against the backdrop of bustling city life, revealing the profound beauty found in solitude and reflection.

Shot over 18 months across Los Angeles, New York, and Chicago, these photographs examine the relationship between individual experience and collective urban energy, finding poetry in the mundane and extraordinary in the overlooked.

Medium: Digital & Film
Year: 2023-2024
Locations: Los Angeles, New York, Chicago
Exhibitions: Gallery Modern, LACMA
